Advanced Renderer

 

Background Information

The Advanced Renderer was created for more complex development and interaction scenarios than the SimpleRenderer component. SimpleRenderer was created for displaying 3D drawings and allowing interaction without events or selection of components within the drawing itself.    

Advanced Renderer was created to allow for the manipulation of a 3D scene within the context of a Salesforce interaction. By default, the component looks similar to the SimpleRenderer, we're drawing is displayed based off of a record ID from salesforce contact version ID from salesforce or a URL directly to t source 3-D file. 

Advanced renderer is extensible, and provides the ability to provide contextual information within our pane system.
