Interacting with our components

Currently, we support Aura interactions, and our components can be wrapped and utilized directly due to their (ever growing) APIs through both attributes, events as well as method. We follow a unidirectional data flow pattern by enabling our rendering components to respond to events, functions and attribute changes, then raising events to respond if data is to be returned. To respond, simply register a handler for that given event type.
