# MeasurePro (iOS App)

MeasurePro by RenderDraw is a cutting-edge room scanning and measurement solution designed for seamless integration with Salesforce. Leveraging **Lidar technology on iOS**, the app allows users to **scan, manage, and import spatial data** directly into their Salesforce environment—even while offline. With an intuitive interface, advanced automation, and direct integration with **RenderDraw’s 2D Canvas**, MeasurePro simplifies the process of capturing and utilizing measurement data for design, quoting, and sales workflows.

***

#### **Purpose & Key Benefits**

**1. Accurate & Effortless Room Scanning**

* Uses Lidar technology for highly precise room measurements.
* Simple, guided scanning process for users of all technical backgrounds.
* Works offline, enabling on-site scans without internet dependency.

**2. Seamless Salesforce Integration**

* Authenticates using Salesforce credentials for a secure and frictionless experience.
* Directly imports scan data into **Lightning Flows**, eliminating manual entry.
* Ensures compatibility with existing Salesforce processes and CPQ solutions.

**3. Enhanced Quoting & Visualization**

* Connects to **RenderDraw’s 2D Canvas** for finalizing drawings and annotations.
* Supports product placement, line-item quoting, and space optimization.
* Enables users to take snapshots, add notes, and refine designs before approval.

**4. Business Efficiency & Productivity**

* Saves time by **automating measurement capture and data import**.
* Reduces errors caused by manual room measuring and entry.
* Improves collaboration by providing immediate, shareable room layouts.

**MeasurePro is the perfect tool for sales, design, and operations teams looking to streamline their space planning and quoting workflows with precision, ease, and full Salesforce compatibility.**
