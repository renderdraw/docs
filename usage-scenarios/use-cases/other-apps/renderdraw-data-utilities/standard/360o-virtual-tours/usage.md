# Usage

{% embed url="https://app.tango.us/app/workflow/3D-Virtual-Tour-User-1fe0ca72754546c6baac860721af2bed" %}

## [3D Virtual Tour User](https://app.tango.us/app/workflow/1fe0ca72-7545-46c6-baac-860721af2bed?utm_source=magicCopy\&utm_medium=magicCopy\&utm_campaign=workflow%20export%20links)

Prior to starting this, ensure a virtual tour has been created by an admin, and your Salesforce users have the "Virtual Tour User" permission set assigned.

***

#### 1. Go to the page where your RenderDraw 3D Virtual Tour has been added

#### 2. Once navigated, we see the interactive screen with hotspots that are clickable. Feel free to take a look around by clicking and dragging your mouse within the window. On mobile, use your finger or pencil/stylus.

![Once navigated, we see the interactive screen with hotspots that are clickable. Feel free to take a look around by clicking and dragging your mouse within the window. On mobile, use your finger or pencil/stylus.](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1fe0ca72-7545-46c6-baac-860721af2bed/steps/b6e1cef0-069f-4bed-809d-98772fa322a9/de0c8a5c-f748-417a-8c08-7eb98a70aa8d.png?fm=png\&crop=focalpoint\&fit=crop\&fp-x=0.4823\&fp-y=0.3150\&fp-z=1.0000\&w=1200\&ar=2624%3A1476)

#### 3. Additionally, you can view a larger window by going fullscreen. Click on the fullscreen button

![Additionally, you can view a larger window by going fullscreen. Click on the fullscreen button](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1fe0ca72-7545-46c6-baac-860721af2bed/steps/026dbe98-ad3c-43a2-be98-0c50516ed8d1/77b3e413-55ab-465e-a619-4dc492be545c.png?fm=png\&crop=focalpoint\&fit=crop\&fp-x=0.5000\&fp-y=0.5000\&fp-z=1.0000\&w=1200\&ar=2624%3A1476)

#### 4. The fullscreen preview will take over the Salesforce screen. Now you can easily navigate to different parts of this tour.

![The fullscreen preview will take over the Salesforce screen. Now you can easily navigate to different parts of this tour.](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1fe0ca72-7545-46c6-baac-860721af2bed/steps/74e04d8f-38c2-401c-a179-3443b544f278/4b1c8125-06f4-40ab-865a-c6baa4377d4e.png?fm=png\&crop=focalpoint\&fit=crop\&fp-x=0.4487\&fp-y=0.4140\&fp-z=1.0000\&w=1200\&ar=2624%3A1476)

#### 5. Once you've navigated your entire tour, exit fullscreen by pressing the fullscreen button in the top right

![Once you've navigated your entire tour, exit fullscreen by pressing the fullscreen button in the top right](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1fe0ca72-7545-46c6-baac-860721af2bed/steps/b7e952e4-3957-4e5b-8e8b-3459df664bf0/d96f0e8e-8aea-412c-bb01-bc16122f9f3b.png?fm=png\&crop=focalpoint\&fit=crop\&fp-x=0.5000\&fp-y=0.5000\&fp-z=1.0000\&w=1200\&ar=2624%3A1476)

#### 6. The fullscreen dialog closes, and we're back where we started! Nice

![The fullscreen dialog closes, and we're back where we started! Nice](https://images.tango.us/workflows/1fe0ca72-7545-46c6-baac-860721af2bed/steps/00d984a3-10ed-4c43-bdd0-9c31be1d25de/e475e243-63d4-4621-9009-9fe460f0cd09.png?fm=png\&crop=focalpoint\&fit=crop\&fp-x=0.7500\&fp-y=0.3735\&fp-z=2.0000\&w=1200\&ar=2624%3A1476)
