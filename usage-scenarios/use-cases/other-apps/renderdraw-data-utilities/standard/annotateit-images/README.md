# AnnotateIt! Images

Ever needed to edit images or PDFs right inside of Salesforce? Yeah, us too..&#x20;

{% embed url="https://youtu.be/MmD_M98Y0fI" fullWidth="true" %}

AnnotateIt was created to allow in-line editing of images throughout Salesforce using Salesforce Files natively.

![](<../../../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (37).png>)

AnnotateIt enables users to&#x20;

1. **Crop**: Cut out unnecessary details, flip or rotate your images and PDF pages.
2. **Filter**: Apply varied filters to improve image quality and clarity.
3. **Finetune**: Modify brightness, contrast, or color for perfect visuals.
4. **Annotate**: Add text, shapes, drawings, and even erase parts of your images or PDF pages.
5. **Stickers**: Incorporate emojis, callouts, or upload your custom designs.
6. **Frame**: Surround your image or PDF pages with a striking border.
7. **Redact**: Secure sensitive data by pixelating specific areas in your document.
8. **Resize**: Adjust image or PDF page sizes to fit your needs.
9. **Whiteboard**: Start fresh with a blank canvas for notes or multiple image use.
10. **Edit on File Details Page**: Make changes to your document right where it resides in Salesforce.
11. **Lightning Flow Ready**: Integrate document editing into your existing workflow.
12. **Admin Configurable**: Customize the editors using Lightning App Builder or Flow Builder.
13. **Chatter Integration**: Keep your team in the loop with automatic Chatter feed updates on annotation text.

Images or PDFs that are hosted within Salesforce, and related to records.
