# About RenderDraw



Embark on a visual journey with RenderDraw, where Salesforce takes on a new dimension. Turn static data into interactive experiences. Whether it's 2D or 3D rendering, CAD conversion, or data utility, RenderDraw has it all. Our unique toolkit allows you to breathe life into Salesforce objects and records, making data engagement not just necessary but also enjoyable.

### Interactive 2D & 3D Renderers

* **2D Canvas**: Unleash the power of interaction! Create drag-and-drop 2D images that interact with your Salesforce objects and records, turning data management into a delightful game.
* **3D Renderers**: Step into the future of data visualization! Explode your products or BIM into interactive 3D, creating a visual spectacle that enchants and informs.
* **CAD Conversion**: Streamline your workflow with our efficient CAD Conversion functionality. Choose between automated conversions for simplicity or manual conversions for the fine-tuning Salesforce admins crave.
